Introduction

Convenient Unit Converter (“the App”) is designed to work primarily offline and to respect user privacy. We are committed to being transparent and minimal in our data practices. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Information We Collect

The App does not automatically collect any personal or sensitive user data.

  • User-entered data: Any information you enter into the App (such as text, numbers, or records) is processed and stored locally on your device only.
  • This data is not transmitted, not uploaded, and not accessible to us.

What we do NOT collect

To ensure total privacy, we explicitly do not collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or location data
  • Usage analytics or crash logs
  • Advertising data or tracking cookies

Data Retention

All data remains on your device and is retained only as long as you keep the App. If you uninstall the App or clear its data, all stored information is permanently deleted. The App does not provide automatic backup or restore functionality; users are responsible for managing their own backups.
